Commercial Cleaning

Initially, the services we provided for our clients centered around the exterior of their properties. However, in 2008 one of our long time customers asked if we would be interested in providing commercial cleaning services for the interior of their office building.
It was a natural and sensible addition to the exterior services we were already providing. Our objective is to make the lives of the property managers we work with as issue and worry free as possible.

Janitorial, Maid and Cleaning Services


Our approach and philosophy to interior commercial cleaning services are exactly the same as our attention to detail on the exterior of the property. Everything must be cleaned exactly as the client expects – nothing left undone and cleaned thoroughly every single visit.
Examples of locations where we provide our janitorial services include, but are not limited to:


  • Office Buildings
  • Apartment Buildings
  • Medical & Dental Offices
  • Retail Stores
  • Schools
  • Hotels
  • Fitness Centers

We are able to staff your location in whatever manner works best for you and your business.


  • Day Time Service
  • After Hours Service
  • Weekend Only Service
  • Daily, Weekly, Monthly

Vacant Space Cleaning & Turnover


We are always working to find new solutions for our commercial property clients. Several years ago a client asked if we would be able to “clean-up a vacant store front”.
Now, whenever a client has a space, whether it be a retail store front, office, apartment, etc., we are able to provide a full range of services to get the space back in order for them so they are able to quickly rent that space again. These services include:


  • Left Behind Trash Removal
  • Broom Swept & Vacuuming
  • Removal of Racks, Shelving, etc.
  • Window Black Out
  • For Lease Signs Hung
  • Cleaning all Common Areas
  • Disinfect and Sanitize
